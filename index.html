About me

Hello, I'm Liss

I'm a digital artist and illustrator based in Belgium, working with clients and publishers around the world. I'm passionate about creating cozy, whimsical characters and sharing creative storylines that spark emotions — and give soul to the things we see.

From children's books and brand identities to characters and packaging, I bring ideas to life with color, texture, and intention. My work is playful but precise, intuitive but thoughtful — always with a touch of magic.

Versatility is my superpower: I shape my style to match each project's heart, whether it needs elegance, boldness, softness, or a little rebellion. I believe illustration isn't just decoration — it's communication, identity, and connection.

If you're here, it's probably because you're dreaming up something special.
Good — I'm here to help you make it real.
